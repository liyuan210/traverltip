Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How often do you update your travel guides?
A: We update our travel guides regularly, typically every 3-6 months, or whenever there are significant changes to destinations, prices, or travel conditions.
Q: Can you help me plan a custom itinerary?
A: While we don't offer personalized itinerary planning services, our comprehensive guides and articles provide detailed information to help you plan your own perfect trip to the Jiangnan region.
Q: Do you accept guest posts or travel stories?
A: We love hearing about your travel experiences! Please contact us with your story ideas, and we'll let you know if they're a good fit for our blog.
Q: How can I stay updated with your latest content?
A: Follow us on social media or bookmark our website to stay updated with the latest travel guides, tips, and destination insights.
Q: Are you affiliated with any travel agencies or hotels?
A: We maintain editorial independence in our content. While we may include affiliate links for reader convenience, our recommendations are always based on genuine experiences and quality.
