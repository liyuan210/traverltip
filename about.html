Our Story

Welcome to Jiangnan Travel Blog, your trusted companion for exploring the enchanting region of Jiangnan in Southern China. Founded with a passion for sharing the beauty and cultural richness of this historic area, we have been helping travelers discover the magic of ancient water towns, exquisite gardens, and culinary delights since our inception.

Jiangnan, literally meaning "south of the river," refers to the fertile and prosperous region south of the Yangtze River. This area, encompassing parts of modern-day Jiangsu, Zhejiang, and Shanghai, has been celebrated in Chinese literature and art for centuries as a land of natural beauty, cultural sophistication, and economic prosperity.
