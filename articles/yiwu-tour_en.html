Explore the unique charm of the world's small commodity capital, experience the collision of tradition and modernity, and feel the fusion of diverse cultures

I. Yiwu: The Unique Charm of the World's Small Commodity Capital

1.1 City Overview and Tourism Value

Yiwu, known as the "World's Supermarket," is famous for being the largest small commodity distribution center globally. By 2025, Yiwu has developed into a highly internationalized commercial city, attracting merchants from over 200 countries and regions who reside here permanently.

Yiwu boasts a commercial area of 6.4 million square meters, with 75,000 booths, 210,000 merchants, and over 2.1 million types of products. It's not just a shopping paradise but also an international metropolis that integrates diverse cultures.

1.2 Best Time to Visit and Climate Characteristics

In late August, Yiwu has a subtropical monsoon climate with temperatures ranging between 25-33°C (77-91°F), with moderate temperature differences between morning and evening, making it very suitable for tourism activities. This period marks the end of summer when the Yiwu International Trade City and major attractions have relatively fewer tourists, making it an ideal time to experience the city.

V. Practical Travel Tips

City Transportation

  • Taxi starting fare is 8 yuan, then about 2.5 yuan per kilometer
  • Bus fare is generally 2 yuan, supporting Alipay and WeChat Pay
  • Free shuttle buses to International Trade City

Shopping Tips

  • Prices at Trade City and night markets usually have room for negotiation, generally can be bargained down to 60%-70% of the marked price
  • International Trade City mainly caters to wholesale customers, but many shops also accept retail

Payment Methods

  • Yiwu merchants generally accept WeChat Pay and Alipay
  • Some large merchants also accept international credit cards
  • It's advisable to prepare some cash as backup
