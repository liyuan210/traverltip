üí° Essential Travel Tips

  • Best Season: Spring (March-May) and Autumn (September-November) offer pleasant weather
  • Advance Booking: Many attractions require online reservation - book through official tourism platforms

  • What to Bring: ID card, power bank, umbrella, and comfortable walking shoes
  • Summer Precautions: Sun protection and typhoon awareness
  • Winter Preparation: Warm clothing for outdoor activities
