Hangzhou & Ningbo 5-Day Deep Travel Guide

Published: January 16, 2025 Author: Jiangnan Travel Expert

The Jiangnan region is renowned worldwide for its profound historical and cultural heritage and beautiful natural scenery. Hangzhou and Ningbo, as two pearls of Jiangnan, each possess unique charm. This 5-day deep travel guide will take you to experience the poetry of West Lake, the Zen spirit of Lingyin Temple, as well as Ningbo's maritime culture and scholarly atmosphere.

II. Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Hangzhou - West Lake Area

Morning: After arriving in Hangzhou, check into your hotel. Then visit West Lake Scenic Area, Hangzhou's iconic attraction, famously described as "West Lake is like a beautiful woman, charming in both light and heavy makeup."

🏞️ West Lake Scenic Area

Rating: ★★★★★

Ticket Price: Free

Visit Duration: About 4 hours

Introduction: West Lake is surrounded by mountains on three sides, with streams flowing into it and numerous springs beneath. This elegant and beautiful lake has been praised by many poets and scholars. The scenic area includes one mountain, two pagodas, three islands, three causeways, ten scenes, and many other natural and cultural landscapes.

Noon: Enjoy Hangzhou specialties at restaurants around West Lake, such as Shrimp and Eel Noodles - smooth noodles, tender shrimp, crispy eel, and delicious flavor.

Afternoon: Visit Yue Fei Temple, a memorial site for the anti-Jin hero Yue Fei.

🏛️ Yue Fei Temple Scenic Area

Rating: ★★★★★

Ticket Price: ¥25

Visit Duration: About 2 hours

Introduction: Yue Fei Temple was first built in the 14th year of Jiading in the Southern Song Dynasty (1221). It was renamed "Zhonglie Temple" during the Jingtai period of Ming Dynasty, and has survived through Yuan, Ming, Qing, and Republican periods.

Evening: Enjoy the night view by West Lake, with the option to watch the "Impression West Lake - Memories of Hangzhou" performance.

Day 2: Hangzhou - Lingyin Temple & Feilai Peak - Faxi Temple

Morning: Visit Lingyin Temple and Feilai Peak Scenic Area, which features numerous stone carvings and ancient temples.

🏔️ Lingyin Temple & Feilai Peak Scenic Area

Rating: ★★★★★

Ticket Price: ¥50 (Lingyin Temple requires separate ticket ¥30)

Visit Duration: About 3 hours

Introduction: The peaks and strange rocks around Lingyin are magnificent, with extraordinary scenery. Indian monk Huili said: "This is a small ridge of Vulture Peak in Central India, but I don't know how it flew here?" Hence the name "Feilai Peak" (Flying Peak).

Afternoon: Visit Faxi Temple (Upper Tianzhu), a famous temple in Hangzhou known for answered prayers, and the birthplace of China's White-robed Guanyin.

🏯 Upper Tianzhu Faxi Temple

Rating: ★★★★

Ticket Price: ¥10

Visit Duration: About 2 hours

Introduction: Faxi Temple was built in the 10th century with over a thousand years of history. The temple has buildings like Sutra Repository, Liangfeng Hall, and Thousand Buddha Pavilion, surrounded by scenic spots like Baiyun Peak and Rudou Peak.

Day 3: Hangzhou - Nine Creeks & Misty Trees - Hangzhou Songcheng

Morning: Visit Nine Creeks & Misty Trees, where beautiful "misty tree" scenery forms after rain, making it a popular family destination.

🌿 Nine Creeks & Misty Trees

Rating: ★★★★

Ticket Price: Free

Visit Duration: About 3 hours

Introduction: Nine Creeks is formed by numerous small streams converging into a "Y"-shaped creek, lined with lush trees and extensive tea gardens.

Afternoon: Visit Hangzhou Songcheng, a theme park that transcends time and challenges imagination, featuring the large-scale song and dance show "Songcheng Eternal Love."

🎭 Hangzhou Songcheng

Rating: ★★★★★

Ticket Price: ¥320

Visit Duration: About 4 hours

Introduction: The main show "Songcheng Eternal Love" is ranked alongside Las Vegas' "O" show and Paris' Moulin Rouge as one of the "World's Top Three Shows." The park features East Street, West Street, North Street, presenting a prosperous Song Dynasty atmosphere.

Day 4: Ningbo - Tianyi Pavilion Museum - Old Bund

Morning: Take the train to Ningbo, then visit Tianyi Pavilion Museum, China's oldest surviving private library.

📚 Tianyi Pavilion Museum

Rating: ★★★★★

Ticket Price: ¥30

Visit Duration: About 3 hours

Introduction: Tianyi Pavilion Museum features book culture as its specialty. The main building, Tianyi Pavilion Library, is one of the world's three earliest surviving private libraries. The park has East Garden and South Garden with rockeries, ponds, and pavilions.

Afternoon: Visit Ningbo Old Bund, one of China's earliest bunds, featuring many European-style buildings and bars.

🌃 Ningbo Old Bund

Rating: ★★★★

Ticket Price: Free

Visit Duration: About 3 hours

Introduction: Old Bund opened in 1844 and is one of the birthplaces of Ningbo's modern commercial culture. It features numerous bars, restaurants, and cafes, making it a great place for leisure and entertainment.

Day 5: Ningbo - Departure

Morning: Free time in downtown Ningbo to purchase local specialties like Ningbo rice cakes and Fenghua honey peaches.

Afternoon: According to your booked train schedule, head to the railway station and take the train back to Shanghai, ending your pleasant journey.

IV. Food Recommendations

Hangzhou Cuisine

🍜 Shrimp and Eel Noodles

Rating: ★★★★★

Price: ¥30-50/bowl

Features: Uses fresh, robust yellow eel, stir-fried with vegetable oil, cooked with lard, and finished with sesame oil until crispy; fresh river shrimp cleaned and coated with egg white, stir-fried until tender and white.

🐔 Beggar's Chicken

Rating: ★★★★

Price: ¥80-150/whole

Features: Uses tender young chicken, marinated with Shaoxing wine and soy sauce, wrapped in pork caul fat, lotus leaves, and bamboo shells, sealed with wine jar clay, and roasted over charcoal fire.

🐟 West Lake Fish in Vinegar Gravy

Rating: ★★★★

Price: ¥50-100/serving

Features: Uses fresh grass carp, first blanched in boiling water, then topped with sweet and sour sauce. The fish meat is tender and the flavor is sweet and sour.

Ningbo Cuisine

🥟 Ningbo Tangyuan (Sweet Rice Balls)

Rating: ★★★★★

Price: ¥10-20/bowl

Features: Made with finely ground glutinous rice flour as skin, filled with lard, white sugar, and black sesame powder. The tangyuan skin is thin and smooth, white as mutton fat, with an oily shine.

🐟 Snow Vegetable with Yellow Croaker

Rating: ★★★★

Price: ¥80-200/serving

Features: Yellow croaker meat is tender and fresh, while snow vegetable is salty and delicious. The combination complements each other perfectly.

🦀 Red Paste Marinated Crab

Rating: ★★★★

Price: ¥100-300/jin

Features: Uses fresh swimming crabs, marinated in salt brine. The crab meat is tender, the roe is full, and the taste is delicious.

Note: As this is raw food, people with sensitive stomachs should consume with caution.

Travel Tips

📅 Best Travel Season: Spring (March-May) and autumn (September-November) are the best times to visit Hangzhou and Ningbo, with pleasant weather and beautiful scenery.

🎫 Ticket Booking: It's recommended to book tickets in advance on official websites or major travel platforms, especially during holidays.

🚄 Transportation Advice: High-speed rail is the most convenient transportation method. It's recommended to book train tickets in advance.

🏨 Accommodation Booking: It's recommended to book hotels in advance, especially during peak seasons. Choose hotels near scenic areas for convenient sightseeing.

🍽️ Food Experience: Don't miss local specialties. You can learn about recommended restaurant locations and business hours in advance.

📱 Useful Apps: It's recommended to download practical apps such as map navigation, weather forecast, and food reviews.

This comprehensive 5-day travel guide for Hangzhou and Ningbo covers transportation, accommodation, attractions, and cuisine, hoping to provide comprehensive reference for your Jiangnan journey. The beautiful scenery of Hangzhou's West Lake and Ningbo's scholarly culture will leave you with unforgettable memories. Have a pleasant trip!
